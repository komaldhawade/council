About GIBF

With the Indian Governments initiatives such as “Make in India", “Skill Development” and “Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (Self-Reliant India Mission)” there are ample opportunities for both Indian and Global companies to run start-ups and make exponential growth in all business sectors. These initiatives also provide a golden opportunity for Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to promote themselves and show their true potential to grow and flourish globally.
As India is spearheading towards becoming the global super power, it is expected that more and more Indian MSMEs explore business opportunities in different countries. Global India Business Forum (GIBF) was formed to show them the opportunities on the global platform, by bringing together business professionals from various segments like Automobile, Engineering, FMCG, Chemical, Pharmaceuticals, Electronics, Heavy Metal, Service Industry etc.
The idea was perceived and progressed further by Dr. Jitendra Joshi, a successful businessman and visionary who has over a glorious 30+ years of experience in Manufacturing and Service Industry at Senior Level Management in numerous MNCs.
GIBF started working on G-100 concept to inform about the business potential in 100 countries through country-wise councils. GIBF also believes that cultural exchange and historical appreciation of each other’s countries build strong bonds that are beneficial for strengthening bilateral trade. Based on this, GIBF has formed Business and Cultural Councils between India and several countries, and is in the process of forming more such councils.
Today, Global India Business Forum is one of the fastest growing networking platforms in the world that has successfully connected businesspersons from different countries and sectors on one platform and is raring to go for more.
We, at GIBF, bridge the gap between demand for opportunities and their supply by conducting webinars, seminars, conclaves, conferences and business delegations. These webinars and conferences help Ambassadors and Consul Generals of countries across the world shed light on several business opportunities their countries have in store for foreign investors. They also help business leaders identify business opportunities that best suit them and we shall help them come together on our platform.
GIBF consists of members from all fields of business, industry, academics and other professions without any segment boundaries.

GIBF has its head office in Delhi, Corporate Office in Pune, India and Global offices in Dubai, USA, UK, Singapore and China. It also has a presence at Mumbai, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Guwahati, Indor, Kolkata and Cuttack in India.
